🌊 The Ship and the Fish

A big ship sat on the sea.
A fish swam past the ship.
The fish had a wish — to find a dish of food.
The fish swam fast. It did not rush.
A man on the ship saw the fish.
He sat and had a wash.
He got some cash and went to the shop.
At the shop, he got a dish.
What a good day on the ship!

🌸 A Chat at the Park

Tom went to the park.
He had a chat with his pal Ben.
Ben had a chip in his bag.
Tom did not have much.
Ben gave Tom a chip.
"What a such big chip!" said Tom.
They sat by a pond.
A duck came up to them.
"What a fun duck!" said Ben.
Tom and Ben had a chat till dusk.
It was such a good day!

📝

1. Where did Tom go?

3. What came up to them at the pond?

🛁 Bath Time!

It is bath time for Maya.
This is fun!
Maya gets in the tub.
She has a thin bar of soap.
She rubs and rubs.
Then she gets out and sits on a mat.
Mum gets a soft cloth.
Math time next!
Maya says the sums in her head.
A moth flew past them.
"Look at that!" said Maya.
Mum said, "Now, bed time!"
Maya went down the path to her room.
What a fun bath!

📝
